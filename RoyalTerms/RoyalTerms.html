(i) Earning Crowns As a Member, you will earn ten (10) Crowns for every $1.00 USD spent (after discounts, excluding applicable taxes and fees) at a participating Burger King® restaurant in the Territory using one of the following methods: (1) on the App or Website for dine-in, pick up or delivery, excluding third-party food delivery aggregator orders; or (2) on in-restaurant orders by using one of the following (i) the 6-digit “My Code” (where available) located in your Account, or (ii) effective January 23, 2023, at participating Burger King® restaurants in the Territory, using a credit card linked to your Account (“Linked Card”) (each an “Eligible Transaction”). You can earn Crowns on up to three (3) Eligible Transactions per Account per day. Your first three (3) Eligible Transactions, regardless of method, will accrue Crowns to your Account. If your Eligible Transaction involves the use of a digital coupon, you will earn Crowns based on actual amount paid. For the avoidance of doubt, you will not get Crowns for the discounted amount of your transaction. Print coupons cannot be used in connection with the Program. If your amount paid includes a decimal, the Crowns earned will be rounded down to the nearest tenth. You will not earn additional Crowns when using Crowns to redeem any reward(s) you earn. If you make a purchase in addition to the use of Crowns during one single transaction, you will earn Crowns on the actual amount paid. An Eligible Transaction paid with your BK Crown gift card will result in Crowns. A transaction made to purchase or reload a BK Crown gift card is not an Eligible Transaction. Crowns earned in connection with an Eligible Transaction will be automatically applied to your Account within 48 hours of completing the Eligible Transaction. For the avoidance of doubt, a transaction completed via a third-party ordering website, a delivery aggregator or other unauthorized means is not an Eligible Transaction. A transaction that does not result in a completed transaction equal or greater than $1.00 USD (after discounts, excluding applicable taxes and fees) is not an Eligible Transaction. If you forget to use either the 6-digit “My Code” located in your Account or your Linked Card during your in-restaurant purchase, please visit bk.com/claim-points to claim your Crowns within 48 hours of purchase. Account registration required. Claiming Crowns via in-restaurant receipt is only available at participating U.S. Burger King® restaurants. Limit one (1) receipt per day is eligible for Crowns. A valid receipt is required to claim your Crowns. Lost, stolen, or misplaced receipts cannot be replaced.
